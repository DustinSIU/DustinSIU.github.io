Directed: Brandyn Mcghee
Technical Director: Dustin Myers
Stage Manager: Jeff Bird



The story follows James Henry Trotter, an orphan boy, who is sent to live with his two tyrannical and scheming aunts. He, through the discovery of a spell book, inadvertently grows a giant peach from the peach tree and brings a spider, centipede, grasshopper, ladybug, and glowing earthworm to human size. Together they escape his awful aunts and journey across the world, learning many wonderful lessons from one another along the way.
